How to Deal With Scam Text Messages

Scam texts often say things like “Your package is on hold” or “Account locked.”

What to Do:

  • 🚫 Don’t click any links
  • 🚫 Don’t reply or call back
  • 📲 Report the message to your carrier by forwarding it to 7726
  • ✅ Block the number in your phone
