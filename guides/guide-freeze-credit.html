How to Freeze Your Credit

Freezing your credit prevents identity thieves from opening accounts in your name. It’s free and takes just a few minutes.

How to Do It:

  • Visit the three major credit bureaus:
    • Equifax
    • Experian
    • TransUnion
  • Verify your identity (SSN, ID, etc.)
  • Place a freeze on your credit reports
  • Keep your PIN secure to unfreeze later
