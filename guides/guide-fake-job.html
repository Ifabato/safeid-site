How to Spot a Fake Job Offer

Fake jobs are common on Indeed, LinkedIn, and even email. Here’s how to protect yourself:

  • ⚠️ They ask for payment up front (equipment, training, shipping)
  • ⚠️ No company website or contact info
  • ⚠️ Unrealistically high salary or quick hire promise
  • ⚠️ Gmail or Yahoo email addresses (not official domains)

✅ Always research the company, request a video interview, and never send money or ID first.
